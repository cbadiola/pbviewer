PB Viewer — Help

PB Viewer visualizes your PBVision game data with coaching-relevant signals. Import your JSON exports, then use the Court Viewer and Dashboard to understand your patterns.

Court Layout

Full court shown
Both halves visible. Your side (left/bottom): muted origin markers showing where you were when you hit. Opponent's side (right/top): impact markers where the ball went. ⚠ Impact positions are estimated by shot type — PBVision does not record ball landing coordinates.
Out-zone bands
Faint red bands outside the court boundary show where out balls land: past the far baseline (long), past right/left sidelines (wide). Direction is from real PBVision data when available.
Serve zone
A faint teal zone behind your baseline shows serve origins. Serves must land past the kitchen line — the service court is from the kitchen line to the far baseline.
Zoom and pan
Scroll wheel zooms toward cursor. Click and drag to pan. Pinch to zoom on iPad. The ⌂ button (bottom-right) resets view. Resize window edge to switch between portrait and landscape layout.

Sidebar Controls

Player
Select which player to analyze. Defaults to Carlo Badiola. The shot count shows how many games include that player.
Outcome filter
In / Net / Out. Filters both impact and origin markers. Net impacts show projected landing location. Out impacts use actual direction data (long/right/left) when tracked.
Shot types
Filter by serve, drive, dink, drop, lob, smash, volley, reset, speed-up, or other. Each type maps to a distinct shape in the legend.
Display layers
Impacts — markers on opponent's half. Origins — markers on your half. Traces — faint lines connecting origin to impact for in-court shots.
Color By
Outcome: green=in, orange=net (projected), red=out. FH/BH: teal=forehand, purple=backhand. Quality: gradient from red (poor) to green (excellent) using PBVision AI scores.
Highlight overlays
Adds dashed rings to matching markers. Retreating (red): you moved away from net >3ft. Advancing (green): moving toward net >3ft. Lateral (purple): wide reach >3ft. High Stress (gold): any movement >6ft.

Game Selection

Partner filter
Chips at the top of the games panel show each partner with your win % when playing with them. Click to filter to only games with that partner.
Date bar chart
Each bar is one calendar month — height = games, green fill = wins. Click any bar to toggle that month on or off. The current (partial) month shows in amber with a ~ marker.
Rolling windows
Buttons above the chart: 2 wks, 4 wks, 8 wks, All. These select by exact date range regardless of calendar month, useful when you're partway through a month.
Individual games
The scrollable list below the chart shows each game. Check or uncheck to include or exclude individual games.

Coaching Insight Box

The floating box (bottom-left of court) shows automatically computed coaching insights ranked by impact. Click ⬆ to expand and see the full diagnosis and drill recommendation. Use ‹ › to cycle through all available insights.

3rd Shot Selection
Compares your drop vs drive accuracy on the third ball and flags if you're choosing the lower-percentage option more often.
Kitchen Transition Gap
Measures accuracy drop between shots 1–3 and shots 4–6 — the window where you should be transitioning to the kitchen.
Retreating Shots
Identifies which shot types you attempt when being pushed back from the net. High retreating drives = attacking from a recovery position.
Poach Discipline
Compares accuracy on poach shots vs standard shots. A large gap suggests over-poaching.

Bottom Stats Bar

The bar at the bottom shows Filtered stats (left) for exactly the shots on screen, Overall record and rating (right), and accuracy/shot-mix tiles in between. Drag the top edge of the bar to resize it.

Shot Shapes

●◉✛⬟ Back-court
Circle = serve (solid). Bull's-eye = drive. Cross-in-circle = lob. Diamond = smash.
■⬜▪ Kitchen
Solid square = dink. Square-in-square = drop. Small square = reset.
▲△ Mid-court
Filled triangle = volley. Outline triangle = speed-up.
× Other / Returns
X mark = unclassified shots (mostly returns of serve).

Dashboard

Win rate, accuracy, kitchen %, and rating KPIs. Rating Over Time chart shows all 7 DUPR sub-scores. Filter by 2-week, 1-month, or all-time. Coaching priorities are from PBVision's AI advice scores ranked by frequency.

Data & Privacy

All data stays in your browser's localStorage. 49 games are pre-loaded in this file. Import additional JSON exports via + Add Games. Export your library with the ⬇ Export button in My Games.

What's Estimated vs Ground Truth

Verified from PBVision data: outcome (in/net/out), out direction (long/right/left), shot type, stroke (FH/BH), player court side, movement magnitude and direction (smov), quality score.

Estimated (zone-based): exact impact landing position, exact origin position. These are approximated using shot-type zones and movement data. Use for clustering and pattern recognition — not precise coordinates.
